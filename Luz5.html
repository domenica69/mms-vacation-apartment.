About the Apartment

M&M's Vacation Apartment is located in a prime area of the Algarve, in Praia da Luz in the Lagos region. Lagos is a historic city in the Algarve, known for its stunning beaches, dramatic cliffs, and charming old town. It is also famous for its sea caves, such as Ponta da Piedade, and has strong ties to the Age of Discoveries. Praia da Luz, is a village located west of Lagos, popular among tourists for its beach and tranquility. From our apartment, we have ocean views and easy access to the best beaches in the region.There is no better way to start the day than with a beautiful sea view, directly from the bedroom or living room of this apartment. Or enjoy the sun and the view on one of the 3 balconies of this apartment. Prainha is a 2-minute walk away and Praia da Luz is 300 meters away. Free public parking is available around the apartment. Supermarket, pharmacy, restaurants, cafes. Everything within a walking distance. No car. It’s perfect for families and couples seeking a relaxing and comfortable holiday.

Local Activities

The village and surrounding area offer a wide range of activities, from water sports to cultural tours. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Stunning beaches: Prainha is a 2-minute walk away and Praia da Luz is 300 meters away. By car, you can easily reach Sagres, Praia da Marinha, and Praia de Benagil.
  • Boat excursions: Explore the caves and rock formations along the Lagos coast.
  • Local cuisine: Taste traditional dishes like seafood cataplana and freshly grilled fish.
  • Golf: Renowned golf courses are just a short drive away.
